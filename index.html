Howdy, friend! The name's Dinky

I'm the mayor of these parts.

You may have seen me around.

I've lived in this apartment complex a long time.

My favorite things to do are:

  1. Lie on top of your car after you get back from a drive.
  2. Chase the squirrels into your patio.
  3. Lie in the middle of your path when you are trying to get to work.

Winter is my slow season. There's not much food on the ground. It gets very cold. During the day I like to sit underneath the dryer vents and take a nap in the hot steam. It makes me smell like clean laundry.

Sometimes I wonder what this place is going to be like when I'm gone. Visions come to me in dreams: the neighbors across the street are moving out. They needed a little more space. A couple moves in. He's a drain technician and parks his red truck in the carport. She's a nurse. The teachers next door to them move out. Two boys move in. They both work at the auto parts store.

Who will remember me when I am gone?

Who will remember to fill my bowl?
